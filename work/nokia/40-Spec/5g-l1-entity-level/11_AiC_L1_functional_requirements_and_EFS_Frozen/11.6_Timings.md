### 11.6.2.0-10 Common Timing Requirements L1 shall use the T1amax - maximum value of API2_BBB_CREATE_EAXC_REQ -&gt; streams -&gt; delayConfigData -&gt; downlikConfig-&gt; tDlAdvanceUp across all eaxcs/ports of the same subcell and of the same channel group (prach, srs, others), to define the L1 reception window. ## 11.6.3 DlData Timings

### 11.6.3.1 SSB Timings

#### 11.6.3.1.0-5 SSB Timings 5G-L1-HI shall process DlData_SsBlockSendReq no longer than 500 us. (ID: 11476845)

### 11.6.3.2 PDSCH Timings

#### 11.6.3.2.0-10 PDSCH Timings 5G-L1-DL expects to receive DlData_PdschPayloadTbSendReq request message between (1000 + T1amax ) us and (300 + T1amax ) us before DL NR-slot boundary. (ID: 11476890)

### 11.6.3.3 PDCCH Timings

#### 11.6.3.3.0-5 PDCCH Timings 5G-L1-DL expects to receive DlData_PdcchSendReq request message between (1000 + T1amax ) us and (500 + T1amax) us before DL NR-slot boundary. (ID: 11476915)

### 11.6.3.4 CSI-RS Timings

#### 11.6.3.4.0-3 CSI-RS Timings 5G-L1-DL expects to receive DlData_CsiRsSendReq request message between (1000 + T1amax ) us and (500 + T1amax ) us before DL NR-slot boundary. (ID: 11476934)

### 11.6.3.5 Other DL Timings

#### 11.6.3.5.0-5 Other DL Timings 5G-L1-DL expects to receive DlData_SlotTypeReq request message between (1000 + T1amax) us and (500 + T1amax) us before DL NR-slot boundary. (ID: 11476960)

## 11.6.4 UlData Timings

### 11.6.4.1 PRACH Timings

#### 11.6.4.1.0-4 PRACH Timings 5G-L1-HI shall support reception timing window for UlData_PrachReceiveReq between (1000 + T1amax)us and (500 + T1amax)us before coresponding DL slot start. (ID: 11476986)

### 11.6.4.2 PUSCH Timings

#### 11.6.4.2.0-12 PUSCH Timings 5G-L1-HI shall send UlData_PuschReceiveRespHarqD between (500 - T_NTA_offset +  receiveWindowClose) us and (1900 - T_NTA_offset + receiveWindowClose) us after UL slot start(UlData_PuschReceiveRespHarqD is only sent when UlData_PuschReceiveReq -&gt; numOfUciAckBits &gt;0). (ID: 11477039)

### 11.6.4.3 PUCCH Timings

#### 11.6.4.3.0-9 PUCCH Timings 5G-L1-HI shall send UlData_PucchReceiveRespHarqD between (500 - T_NTA_offset +  receiveWindowClose) us and (1900 - T_NTA_offset + receiveWindowClose) us after UL slot start(UlData_PucchReceiveRespHarqD is not sent when UlData_PucchReceiveReq -&gt; nANPucch = 0). (ID: 11477078)

### 11.6.4.4 SRS Timings

#### 11.6.4.4.0-4 SRS Timings 5G-L1-HI shall send UlData_SrsReceiveRespPs between (500 - T_NTA_offset + receiveWindowClose)us and (2500 - T_NTA_offset + receiveWindowClose) us after UL slot start. (ID: 11477099)

## 11.6.5 eCPRI Timings

### 11.6.5.0-1 eCPRI Timings 5G-L1-HI shall send SymbolEvent that are containing FCP payload no later than 500us + tDlAdvanceUp before starting the radio slot to which it shall be  applied. (ID: 11477107)
