# 9.2 UL Bandwidth Configurations

**9.2.0-1**  (ID: `13346434`)

UL Bandwidth Configurations
5G-L1-UL (FHE-CPRI-GW) shall support the following UL bandwidth configuration for FR1 FDD:

* UL bandwidth is provided by bandwidth in API\_BBB\_CREATE\_EAXC\_AXC\_ROUTING\_REQ\_MSG  (with direction is "EBbbEaxcDirection\_Uplink")
* Sample Rate is provided by iqSampleCount in API\_BBB\_CREATE\_EAXC\_AXC\_ROUTING\_REQ\_MSG  (with direction is "EBbbEaxcDirection\_Uplink")
