**13.3.5.2.1.0-2**  (ID: `11401731`)

L1-SW and L1-FPGA shall support Algorithm changes introduced by 5GC002006 Part 2:

* PUSCH FOC update

-- Internal to L1-FPGA. The inter symbol FOC, intra symbol FOC and OFDM phase compensation are merged together and are applied between TOC and Channel estimation.

* PUSCH Cst calculation update

-- Internal to L1-FPGA. To reduce the complexity without performance loss, the channel estimation for PTRS is used in short term correlation calculation.

-- L1-SW should read from configuration files the parameter pneRbThreshold and write it to L1-FPGA.

-- L1-FPGA should define to L1-SW how these tables should be written.

-- Note that the parameter pneRbThreshold depends on the rank and antenna configuration.
