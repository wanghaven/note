# 4.21 Successful delivery indication of RRC Message

## 4.21.0-2 Successful delivery indication of RRC Message In certain cases, CPlane may request successful delivery of the PDCP PDU containing RRC message. On reception of RLC STATUS PDU with successful acknowledgement for the RLC PDU corresponding to the PDCP PDU for which confirmation of successful delivery was requetsed by CPlane 5G-L2-HI shall send the indication to CPlane confirming the successful delivery.
