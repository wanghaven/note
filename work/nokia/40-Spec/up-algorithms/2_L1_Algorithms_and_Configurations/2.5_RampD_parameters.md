## 2.5.0-7 R&amp;D parameters rdRtBwvDowngradeMethod (ID: 11110305)

**2.5.0-7.0-1**  (ID: `11110307`)

R&D parameters
This parameter is used to select method of SRS BWVs downgrading for short term BF

Value range: {0, 1, 2, 3, 4}

0. Baseline, dropped SRS ports if corresponding quality is lower than srsQualityThreshold (see 5G\_UP\_ALG\_13821\_replaced([11094605](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kmeS5vvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ))), the number of BWV = min {number of valid SRS ports, number of BWV per subband requested by L2-PS}.

1. The number of BWVs is aligned with the request from 5G-L2-PS, until averaged SRS quality of those SRS ports is lower than srsQualityThreshold. (for example, if L2-PS indicates 3 SRS ports, L1 would drop 1 SRS port with the lowerest power and calculate the average power of the remaining SRS ports)

2. The 2 BWVs constantly, until averaged SRS quality of those 2 SRS ports is lower than srsQualityThreshold. (for test only)

3. The 3 BWVs constantly, until averaged SRS quality of those 3 SRS ports is lower than srsQualityThreshold.  (for test only)

4. The 4 BWVs constantly, until averaged SRS quality of 4 SRS ports is lower than srsQualityThreshold.  (for test only)

Default value: 1

Unit: -

Online modifiable: Yes

Step size: -

Specified in: 5G\_UP\_ALG\_13935\_replaced([11107463](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kofsJfvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ))

Trigger for deletion: Never.

Note: This parameter is applied on Loki and [CB008322-F]Thor [End CB008322-F]

Owner:  Xu, Stone (NSB - CN/Hangzhou)
