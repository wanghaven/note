Signaling Overload flow from 5G-L2-PS perspective.

The procedure is as follow:

Steps:

1. (10) The signaling load is greater than the threshold **[Before CB013709-C]configured via rdNbProcedureContextsHigh** [CB013709-C] (See 5G\_L2\_21783\_replaced([11214405](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_2-yRi_vTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) for detailed logic behind threshold) **[End CB013709-C]  for a particular L2Pool.**\*\*\*\*
2. (20) 5G-L2-PS shall trigger the message PsCell\_OverloadProtectionReq to inform that L2Pool is overloaded.
3. (30) 5G-CP-RT shall trigger the message PsCell\_OverloadProtectionResp to acknowledges the overload information.
4. (40) 5G-CP-RT shall stop sending PsUser procedures if FAF for overload on the 5G-CP-RT side is true.
5. (50) The current incoming signaling load is greater than the threshold configured **[Before CB013709-C] via rdNbProcedureContextsMaxLimit.** [CB013709-C]  (See 5G\_L2\_21783\_replaced([11214405](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_2-yRi_vTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) for detailed logic behind threshold) **[End CB013709-C] 5G-L2-PS shall drop new and future PsUser procedures.**\*\*\*\*
6. (60) The current incoming signaling load is less than the threshold configured  **[Before CB013709-C] via rdNbProcedureContextsLow** **[CB013709-C]  (See 5G\_L2\_21783\_replaced([11214405](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_2-yRi_vTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) for detailed logic behind threshold)** [End CB013709-C]  for L2Pool which is in overload state.***\****\*
7. (70) 5G-L2-PS shall send PsCell\_OverloadProtectionReq to inform that L2Pool is not overloaded.
8. (80) 5G-CP-RT shall trigger the message PsCell\_OverloadProtectionResp to acknowledges the overload state is over.
9. (90) 5G-CP-RT shall stop overload action and start sending new PsUser procedures.
10. (100) 5G-L2-PS shall proceed new PsUser procedures.

The sequence of events is as follows:
1. **5G-L2-PS** detects an overload state.

8. **5G-CP-RT** acknowledges that the overload state is over by sending a "PsCell\_OverloadProtectionResp" message.
9. **5G-CP-RT** starts sending PsUser procedures again.
10. **5G-L2-PS** also starts proceeding with PsUser procedures.

Scheduling Overload flow from 5G-L2-PS perspective.

The procedure is as follow:

Steps:

1. (10)  **[Before CB013709-C] The averaged CS1 max in DL/UL is less than the threshold due to processing overload and the number of UEs with data in DL/UL buffer is high by comparing against the thresholds (refer to 5G\_L2\_21790\_replaced([11214469](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_2-y4n_vTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ))).** [CB013709-C] 5G-L2-PS detects the scheduling overload - see 5G\_L2\_21785\_replaced([11214423](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_2-yRjvvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) for algorithm references **[End CB013709-C]**\*\*\*\*
2. (20) 5G-L2-PS shall trigger the message PsCell\_OverloadProtectionReq to inform that cell is overloaded. In case Downlink/Uplink Scheduling Overload happens after Uplink/Downlink Scheduling Overload, then L2-PS shall not indicate 5G-CP-RT. 5G-L2-PS shall log the occurrence of every overload condition that is met separately irrespective of the indication that is sent to 5G-CP-RT.
3. (30) 5G-CP-RT shall trigger the message PsCell\_OverloadProtectionResp to acknowledges the overload information.
4. (40) 5G-CP-RT shall stop sending new PsUser procedures if FAF for overload on the CP-RT side is true.
5. (50) The overload exit criteria for DL and UL are met by comparing against threshold values **[Before CB013709-C] (refer to 5G\_L2\_21790\_replaced([11214469](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_2-y4n_vTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ))).** [CB013709-C] - see 5G\_L2\_21785\_replaced([11214423](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_2-yRjvvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) for algorithm references ***\*[End CB013709-C]*****\***
6. (60) 5G-L2-PS shall send PsCell\_OverloadProtectionReq to inform that cell is not overloaded.
7. (70) 5G-CP-RT shall trigger the message PsCell\_OverloadProtectionResp to acknowledges the overload state is over.
8. (80) 5G-CP-RT shall stop overload action and start sending new PsUser procedures.
9. (90) 5G-L2-PS shall proceed PsUser procedures.
