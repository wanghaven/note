# 5.1 General

**5.1.0-5**  (ID: `11240919`)

General
5G-L2-SRB shall perform PDCP reestablishment

5G-L2-SRB shall perform on receiving SrbUser\_BearerModifyReq with reestablishPdcp = TRUE,

a) Discard the UL PDCP SDU present in the re-ordering buffer.

b) Discard the stored DL PDCP SDU(s)/PDU(s).

c) Initialise PDCP Tx and Rx state variables. Stop all PDCP timers.

d) Continue the DL and UL operation as per 5G\_L2\_2792\_replaced([11240936](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_3BiDi_vTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) and 5G\_L2\_2794\_replaced([11240950](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_3BiDjfvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).
