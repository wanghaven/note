#### 6.5.3.1.1 CSI-RS for tracking RE level rate matching

**6.5.3.1.1.0-1**  (ID: `12565112`)

CSI-RS for tracking RE level rate matching
If PDSCH and CSI-RS for tracking are allocated to the same OFDM-symbols and same PRBs, PDSCH is rate-matched around the REs of CSI-RS for tracking (TDD FR1 and FDD). The REs for CSI-RS shall be jumped over when allocating REs for PDSCH.

The positions of REs for CSI-RS are computed based on *startSymbol, startPrb, numOfPrb, freqDomainAllocationKi, csiRsConfig and density in* DlDataFH\_CsiRsSend*Req message ([11516686](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FSF45vvxEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) for eCPRI-7-2e.*\*\*

If PDSCH and CSI-RS are transmitted on the different subcells, an extra *DlDataFH\_CsiRsSendReq message ([11516686](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FSF45vvxEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) for eCPRI-7-2e**shall be sent to the subcell where PDSCH is transmitted in order to get the positions of REs for CSI-RS, but the power of CSI-RS within this subcell is muted through* pwrReductionPerCsiRsResource\_dB IE.\*\*\*\*
