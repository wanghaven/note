## 3.9.2 UE number

### 3.9.2.0-1 UE number Max UE number per Cell is limited by L1 CSI report capacity for CQI, refer to 11609546 , 13450975 . UE capacity in a single L2-RT includes two types: maxNbPcellUePerL2rt maxNbUePerL2rt The value of maxNbUePerL2rt cannot exceed maxNbPcellUePerL2rt. So, in the following description, UE capacity refers to maxNbPcellUePerL2rt. According to 11609546 , 13450975 maxNbPcellUePerL2rt = MIN (maxTargetNbPcellUePerL2rt; l1MaxRcuCapacity; l2MaxRcuCapacity), with maxTargetNbPcellUePerL2rt = MAX (maxNbOfRcuPerCell * nbOfCellsPerL2rt) ** In the following parts, l1MaxRcuCapacity limitation are not considered. So, the value of maxNbPcellUePerL2rt equals MIN (maxTargetNbPcellUePerL2rt, l2MaxRcuCapacity) . ** (ID: 11217694)

## 3.9.3 DRB and SRBs Capacity

### 3.9.3.0-2 DRB and SRBs Capacity DRB and SRB number per L2LO instance (ID: 11217825)

**3.9.3.0-2.0-2**  (ID: `11217835`)

DRB and SRBs Capacity According to 5G_RAN_ARC_8018_replaced([11608399](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FZew_fwKEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)), for the Max DRBs per UE, NSA is 8 and SA is 16, but the average DRBs per UE is 2 for both Classical and vDU.

According to 5G_RAN_ARC_8017_replaced([11608366](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FZew9vwKEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)), Max SRB per NSA UE is 1 and max SRB per SA UE is 2.

So, [CB012704-A] in Classic,[End CB012704-A] the DRB capacity per L2-RT is calculated as maxNbPcellUePerL2rt * 2, and the SRB capacity per L2-RT equals to the value of maxNbPcellUePerL2rt(NSA) or maxNbPcellUePerL2rt * 2(SA).

[CB012704-A] in VDU, the DRB capacity per L2-RT is calculated as (Max number of UE) * 2, and the SRB capacity per L2-RT equals to the value of (Max number of UE) (NSA) or (Max number of UE) * 2(SA). [End CB012704-A]

For maxNbPcellUePerL2rt [CB012704-A] and Max number of UE[End CB012704-A], refer to 5G_L2_3378_replaced([11217687](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_2_FzhPvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).

**3.9.4.1.1.0-2.0-6**  (ID: `13420565`)

Common According to [11609814](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FZsMUPwKEe-AqvopbP1qhQ?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9), [13450975](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_BpB_EikOEfGxldjOZQLezQ?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9) , following configurations are used in L2LO as typical peak Throughput definition:

 2 DRBs per UE

 In TDD FR1 and FDD FR1, as many UE as SDM per covered cell in case of MU-MIMO

\- Scheduled UE per TTI per Loki for FDD FR1 is 38 on Downlink and 38 on Uplink

\- Scheduled UE per TTI per Loki for TDD FR2 is 8 on Downlink and 4 on Uplink

\- Scheduled UE per TTI per half Thor for TDD FR1 is 48 on Downlink and 48 on Uplink

\- Scheduled UE per TTI per half Thor for FDD FR1 is 50 on Downlink and 46 on Uplink

\- Scheduled UE per TTI per half Thor for TDD FR2 is 8 on Downlink and 4 on Uplink

\- Scheduled UE per TTI per half RINLINE2 for TDD FR1 is 48 on Downlink and 48 on Uplink

\- Scheduled UE per TTI per full RINLINE2 1 pool for TDD FR1 is 80 on Downlink and 96 on Uplink

\- Scheduled UE per TTI per half RINLINE2 for FDD FR1 is 50 on Downlink and 46 on Uplink
