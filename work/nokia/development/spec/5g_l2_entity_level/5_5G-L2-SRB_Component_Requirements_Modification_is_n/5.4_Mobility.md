# 5.4 Mobility

**5.4.0-2**  (ID: `11241034`)

Mobility
5G-L2-SRB shall handle PDCP reestablishment due to key change

5G-L2-SRB shall perform on receiving SrbUser\_SecurityConfReq with reestablishPdcp = TRUE,

a) Discard the UL PDCP SDU present in the re-ordering buffer.

b) Discard the stored DL PDCP SDU(s)/PDU(s).

c) Initialise PDCP Tx and Rx state variables. Stop all PDCP timers.

d) Store and use the Ciphering and Integrity Protection keys received in SrbUser\_SecurityConfReq.

e) Continue the DL and UL operation as per 5G\_L2\_2792\_replaced([11240936](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_3BiDi_vTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) and 5G\_L2\_2794\_replaced([11240950](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_3BiDjfvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).
