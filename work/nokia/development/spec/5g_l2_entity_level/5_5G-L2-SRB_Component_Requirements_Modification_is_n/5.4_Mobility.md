# 5.4 Mobility

## 5.4.0-2 Mobility 5G-L2-SRB shall handle PDCP reestablishment due to key change 5G-L2-SRB shall perform on receiving SrbUser_SecurityConfReq with reestablishPdcp = TRUE, a) Discard the UL PDCP SDU present in the re-ordering buffer. b) Discard the stored DL PDCP SDU(s)/PDU(s). c) Initialise PDCP Tx and Rx state variables. Stop all PDCP timers. d) Store and use the Ciphering and Integrity Protection keys received in SrbUser_SecurityConfReq. e) Continue the DL and UL operation as per 5G_L2_2792_replaced( 11240936 ) and 5G_L2_2794_replaced( 11240950 ).
