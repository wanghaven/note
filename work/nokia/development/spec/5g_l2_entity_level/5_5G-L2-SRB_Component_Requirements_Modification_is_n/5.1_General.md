# 5.1 General

## 5.1.0-5 General 5G-L2-SRB shall perform PDCP reestablishment 5G-L2-SRB shall perform on receiving SrbUser_BearerModifyReq with reestablishPdcp = TRUE, a) Discard the UL PDCP SDU present in the re-ordering buffer. b) Discard the stored DL PDCP SDU(s)/PDU(s). c) Initialise PDCP Tx and Rx state variables. Stop all PDCP timers. d) Continue the DL and UL operation as per 5G_L2_2792_replaced( 11240936 ) and 5G_L2_2794_replaced( 11240950 ). (ID: 11240919)
