## 14.1.2 Addressing Procedure

**14.1.2.0-1**  (ID: `11207606`)

Addressing Procedure
To be able to interact with L1-DL and L1-UL for L1 pools and sub-pools management, 5G-L2 has to perform an initial address exchange procedure towards L1-DL and L1-UL pool interfaces described in L1\_IF\_14427 and L1\_IF\_14430.

* Concerning this exchange between L2 and L1-DL, the corresponding data flow is presented in REQ: 5G\_L1\_IF\_14848\_replaced([11513326](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FRv6qPvxEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).
* Concerning the similar exchange between L2 and L1-UL, the data flow is presented in REQ: 5G\_L1\_IF\_14822\_replaced([11515353](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FR7g3vvxEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).

These exchanges are initiated by L2-PS schedulers in the cell setup sequences as presented in the data flow in REQ: 5G\_L2\_4164\_replaced([11246025](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_3CAkqvvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).

## 14.1.3 Messaging Procedures

### 14.1.3.1 L1 pools and sub pools reconfiguration

**14.1.3.1.0-1**  (ID: `11207634`)

L1 pools and sub pools reconfiguration
In the context of PRB pooling features, it is necessary to reconfigure the L1 pools. In particular, the L1 subpool sizes of a L1 pool might be adjusted. In order to do so, the following exchanges have to be used:

* For the reconfiguration of the L1 Downlink pools: see data flow presented in REQ: 5G\_L1\_IF\_14851\_replaced([11513349](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FRv6rfvxEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).
* For the reconfiguration of the L1 Uplink pools: see data flow presented in REQ: 5G\_L1\_IF\_14832\_replaced([11515375](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_FR7g4_vxEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).

These exchanges are initiated by BBRM entity in L2-PS as presented in the data flow in REQ: 5G\_L2\_7758\_replaced([11246481](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_3CDA7fvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).
