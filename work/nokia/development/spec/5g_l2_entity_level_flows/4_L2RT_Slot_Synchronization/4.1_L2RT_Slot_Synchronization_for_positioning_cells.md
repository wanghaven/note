**4.1.1.2.0-2.0-1**  (ID: `11177552`)

Description:
Loop until Aa5GTimeCheckIfSyncDone() returns GLO\_TRUE or procedure interrupt i.e. due to Positioning Cell setup timeout

#### 4.1.1.2.0-5 Description: (5) Timer wait for synchro starts (ID: 11177585)

**4.1.1.2.0-5.0-1**  (ID: `11177590`)

## 4.1.2 L2RT Slot Synchronization for positioning cell timing

### 4.1.2.0-1 L2RT Slot Synchronization for positioning cell timing Slot Synchronization for positioning cell timing (ID: 11177675)

**4.1.2.0-1.0-1**  (ID: `11177682`)

L2RT Slot Synchronization for positioning cell timing
5G-L2-PS shall follow the 5G\_L1L2\_FLOW\_672 except the 5G-L2-LO part shall be omitted. Rationale: 5G-L2-LO is not used in L2RT deployment for positioning cells, see 5G\_L2\_19071\_replaced([11140277](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_23iaCPvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).
