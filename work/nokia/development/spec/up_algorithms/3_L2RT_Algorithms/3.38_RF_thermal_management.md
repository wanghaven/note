# 3.38 RF thermal management

## 3.38.0-1 RF thermal management RF thermal management mechanism (ID: 13327720)

**3.38.0-1.0-1**  (ID: `13327622`)

RF thermal management There are two methods for RF thermal management from 27R2 onward:

1\. Slot blanking.

2\. MIMO muting with hopping.

L2-PS shall perform slot blanking ([11175086](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kugNkvvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)) or rank limitation due to MIMO muting with hopping ([11139212](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_krlcZvvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)) according to RF thermal management indicator ([13333900](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_GgklQNaaEfCnq6aYkXj8Pw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)) from CPRT.
