UE Throughput Counters Burst volume calculation

---

**3.35.0-3.0-2.0-2**  (ID: `13337497`)

UE Throughput Counters Burst volume is defined as the sum of DL MAC SDU volumes transmitted by gNB-DU across all bursts across all bearers that belong to one cell over a measurement period. see the figure in [12481582](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_bOxisQ9lEfCvOvZoOJBSnQ) for an example.

[CNI-156478-A] For burst identification, please refer to [12481578: Identify a burst](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_8x04EA9kEfCvOvZoOJBSnQ?oslc_config.context=https%3A%2F%2Fdn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com%2Fgc%2Fconfiguration%2F9) [End CNI-156478-A]

Notes

1\. Last transport block (TB) of the burst will be excluded from the burst volume.

2\. HARQ retransmissions of TBs are not included in MAC SDU volume, only initial transmissions are considered.

3\. gNB-DU shall start burst volume calculation after the 1st transport block is transmitted over the air interface.

UE Throughput Counters Discard a burst

---

**3.35.0-3.0-4.0-1**  (ID: `12481586`)

UE Throughput Counters If burst volume is less than NRBTS.NRDRB.volThresDiscardDlMacSdu or burst duration is shorter than NRBTS.NRDRB.durationThresDiscardDlMacSdu, then statistic of the burst is discarded, that is, neither burst volume nor burst duration will be collected by gNB-DU for such burst. In FR2 cells, the discard parameters are applicable for the merged burst but not for the cell specific sub-bursts.

## 3.35.0-5 UE Throughput Counters Cell Specific Volume Counters (ID: 11217393)

**3.35.0-5.0-1**  (ID: `11217400`)

UE Throughput Counters Counters when no S-Cells are configured

When no S-Cells are configured and an RLC PDU is transmitted to an UE in the DL, the 5G-L2-LO shall update the counter which provides the DL data volume sent through this cell to UEs that doesn’t have any S-Cell(s) configured.

Counters at Pcell for UEs when they have atleast one Scell configured

When atleast one S-Cell is configured, the 5G L2-LO at a Pcell shall maintain the following counters: 

  * The counter that provides the DL data volume for the UEs that use this cell as P-Cell for CA. This counter is applicable for both FR1 and FR2 and is updated when an RLC PDU is transmitted to UEs from the P-Cell.
  * The counter that provides the DL data volume sent from the P-Cell to the S-Cells for those UEs that use this cell as P-Cell for CA. This counter is applicable only for FR2 and is updated when the RLC PDU is transmitted to an S-Cell from the P-Cell.



Counter at Scell when it is activated

The 5G-L2-LO of an S-Cell shall maintain the counter that provides the DL data volume for the UEs that use this cell as S-Cell for CA. This counter is applicable for both FR1 and FR2 and is updated when an RLC PDU is transmitted to the UE from the S-Cell.

Note 1: All the above counters shall be applicable for both SA and NSA.

Note 2: When at least one Scell is enabled, the counters at Pcell and Scell shall cover both Inter-gNB & Intra-gNB CA.

## 3.35.0-6 UE Throughput Counters Burst Handling for throughput counters**** (ID: 11217408)

**3.35.0-6.0-3**  (ID: `12481596`)

UE Throughput Counters Identifying a data burst

If the flag NRBTS.actMacUserThpt is true, then [Before CB013789-SR-E] for each bearer, the 5G-L2-LO shall identify the start of a burst when the first PDU from 5G-L2-HI layer reaches 5G-L2-LO. On the other hand, the 5G-L2-LO shall identify the end of burst when the last TB of the data is sent over air to UE. Note that the buffer at L2-LO is considered empty when the payload for the last TB of the DL MAC SDU burst has completed building at L2-LO. [CB013789-SR-E] gNB-DU shall identify the data burst for each bearer as per [12481578](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_8x04EA9kEfCvOvZoOJBSnQ). [End CB013789-SR-E]

Reporting to 5G-L2-HI

[Before CB013789-SR-E]

For an FR1 cell, when NRBTS.actMacUserThpt is TRUE and a burst has been identified, 5G-L2-LO shall send the following parameters to 5G-L2-HI per bearer per burst:

  * The point in time when the first DL MAC SDU of the burst is received at 5G-L2-LO from 5G-L2-HI.
  * The point in time when the first TB of the DL MAC SDU burst is transmitted over air to UE.
  * The point in time when the second last TB of the DL MAC SDU burst is transmitted over air to UE.
  * The point in time when the payload built was completed at L2LO for the last TB of the DL MAC SDU burst.
  * The point in time when the last TB of the DL MAC SDU burst is transmitted over air to UE.
  * The total volume of all MAC SDUs in the burst
  * The volume of DL MAC SDUs in last TB.



For an FR1 cell, each P-Cell and S-Cell(s) have different buffers, thus the above parameters are reported per cell to L2-HI. An illustration of the reported parameters is given in 5G_UP_ALG_25576_replaced([11217446](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kxepC_vSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).

For FR2 cells, irrespective of the number of component carriers, a single buffer is maintained. Thus, when NRBTS.actMacUserThpt is TRUE and a burst has been identified, 5G-L2-LO shall calculate

  * UE throughput counters for the merged burst at 5G-L2-LO if NRBTS.actPerCellMacUserThpt is TRUE. This involves calculating the total RLC PDU volume and time needed to transmit that corresponding volume for the merged burst. The merging of the bursts shall follow the exact same procedure followed by gNB-DU (5G-L2-HI) for FR1 depicted in 5G_UP_ALG_19549_replaced([11228573](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kyjACvvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) and the calculation of volume and time for throughput for the burst shall be based on 5G_UP_ALG_19487_replaced([11217497](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kxfQEfvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)). Additionally, the volume and time counters at 5G-L2-LO shall also be calculated per PMQAP profile.
  * Per CC UE throughput counters based on the merged burst if NRBTS.actPerCCDLUserThpt is TRUE. Whenever CC combination changes (i.e. when a local burst at P(S)cell starts/ends), 5G-L2-LO shall calculate time of each CC combination based on overlapping. The volume of each CC combination is then calculated using (time duration of each CC combination)* (throughput for each CC combination) . This counter is further explained in 5G_UP_ALG_25565_replaced([11217487](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kxepEfvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).



Notes:

1\. The above parameters are only sent for initial transmissions. Retransmissions are not considered.

2\. For FR2 cells, small volume and short session bursts (as configured in NRBTS.NRDRB.volThresDiscardDlMacSdu & NRBTS.NRDRB.durationThresDiscardDlMacSdu) are excluded from the counting. The discard parameters are applicable for the merged burst and not for the cell specific sub-bursts.

3\. For FR2 cells, volume & time of the last TB of the merged burst that empties the buffer is excluded from the UE throughput counters. 

[CB013789-SR-E]

In FR1 cell, when a burst has been identified, if NRBTS.actMacUserThpt is TRUE, L2-LO shall send burst statistics to L2-HI according to [12481592](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_lWjHIA9pEfCvOvZoOJBSnQ).

[End CB013789-SR-E]

## 3.35.0-8 UE Throughput Counters Per CC Throughput Counters in the DL (ID: 11217487)

**3.35.0-8.0-1**  (ID: `11217491`)

UE Throughput Counters For FR2, when NRBTS.actPerCCDLUserThpt is TRUE and there in non-GBR traffic in the DL, the 5G-L2-LO shall provide volume and time counters for each CC combination at RLC PDU level based on the merged burst. These volume and time counters shall be updated in the PCell. The per CC volume and time counters for each CC combination will then be used to update the following counters

  * Sum (DL non-GBR UE Volume for UEs with 1 carrier … DL non-GBR UE Volume for CA Active UEs with 8CC)
  * Sum (DL non-GBR UE Time for UEs with 1 carrier … DL non-GBR UE Time for CA Active UEs with 8CC)

## 3.35.0-9 UE Throughput Counters DL MAC SDU Throughput Counter Per Cell at 5G-L2-LO (ID: 11217497)

**3.35.0-9.0-3**  (ID: `12483264`)

UE Throughput Counters 12483264: UE Throughput Counters [Before CB013789-SR-E]

If the flag NRBTS.actPerCellMacUserThpt is true, then 5G-L2-LO calculates the following counters.

Volume Counter

This is defined as the sum of DL MAC SDU volumes transmitted by gNB-DU across all bursts across all bearers that belong to one cell over a measurement period.

Time Counter

This counter computes the sum of duration of all bursts of all bearers that belong to a cell in the measurement period. Note that for each burst, the duration of the burst is calculated by the excluding the initial scheduling and retention delay.
