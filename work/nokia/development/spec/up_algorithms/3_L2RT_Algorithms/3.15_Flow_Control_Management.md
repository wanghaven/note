###### 3.16.2.1.1-3.0-1 Contention Bassed and Contention Free RA preambles Computation of Accumulated Timing offset (ID: 13469973)

**3.16.2.1.1-3.0-1.0-1**  (ID: `13477925`)

Contention Bassed and Contention Free RA preambles gNB-DU shall maintain the NTA computation for every permanent UE context. The accumulated timing offset is used to calculate UE's position.

The accumulated timing offset is sum of:

  * RACH initial TA calculated for positioning during PRACH reception 

  * All timing adjustments carried in Timing advance command MAC-CE sent to UE 

  * Long Term Timing offset measurement 




Initialization of NTA

NTA is initialized with the result “Timing Advance measurement value” is Tadv measurement ([11152513](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_ksgpdfvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)), ([11152489](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_ksgpcfvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ))), which is in unit of Tc (TS 38.211 4.1).

Computation of NTAiin Tc unit shall be computed as and when TAC in MAC-CE is sent to UE and acknowledged positively (Harq Ack) as follows

**N** TA **Pos[ue] = N** TA**Pos[ue, t-1] + (TAC [ue] - 31)_16 * 64/2µ_**** _*_**

Computation of Accumulated Timing offset for positioning

gNB-DU shall compute accumulated timing offset (Tc unit) for positioning when it has to report it to higher layer

**rrmAccumulatedTimingOffsetPos = N** TA **Pos[ue] + rrmUeLongTermTimingOffset[ue] * 64/2μ** ****

with

ue: Index of 5G UE

NTAPos:(ue,t-1]: Previous value of NTAPos 

TAC: The TA value carried in TAC MAC-CE ([11150271: TAC computation](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/TX_C-kW5_vMEe-AqvopbP1qhQ))

μ: SCS of PRACH

rrmUeLongTermTimingOffset: Long Term Timing offset ([11150211](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/TX_CtPWUPvMEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) - It shall be added when reporting rrmAccumulatedTimingOffsetPos to the higher layer only when the value is non-zero and not "Invalid".

Rationale for adding long term timing offset only while reporting is that it is reset to 0 when TAC CE is sent to UE and Harq Acked. During reporting to the higher layer if long term timing offset is non-zero and considering the same will increase the precision.

[CB014535-SR-A-UPD002] When the higher layer requests accumulated TA, gNB-DU computes the accumulated timing offset for positioning measurements, it shall record the SFN, slot, and relative timestamp from January 1, 1970. SFN and slot for accumulated TA shall be updated when any of the following is received for the UE

  * PRACH reception SFN, slot
  * PUSCH reception SFN, slot (which is used for computing rrmUeLongTermPuschTimingOffset)
  * PUCCH F3 reception SFN, slot (which is used for computing rrmUeLongTermPucchTimingOffset)



This recorded information shall be reported to the higher layer along with accumulated timing advance measurement.[End CB014535-SR-A-UPD002]

##### 3.16.5.1.0-1 CBRA contention resolution (MAC CE CRI or Uplink grant) Contention resolution procedures (ID: 11152993)

**3.16.5.1.0-1.0-4**  (ID: `13462085`)

CBRA contention resolution (MAC CE CRI or Uplink grant) L2 PS shall handle a contention resolution timer for each UE during a contention based random access procedure. Thus, the timer shall be started in the slot which is indicated for RA-Msg3 transmission, see also 5G_UP_ALG_4627_replaced([11192210](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kvivXPvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)), and restarted in each slot that is scheduled for a retransmission of RA-Msg3, see 5G_UP_ALG_5096_replaced([11203473](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kwfKhPvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).

The timer contResTimer, started in the UL scheduler for the SFN:Slot of the PUSCH, is expressed in slots and shall be initialized to:

contResTimer = NRCELL/raContentionResolutionTmr x 2^µ

With,

µ :

Numerology according to the common SCS (15kHz: 0, 30kHz: 1, 120kHz: 3)

The 5G-L2-PS behavior is :

 For CBRA with RA-Msg3[CRNTI MAC CE] (ex: NSA call setup(CBRA), HO(CBRA)...):

 If a DCI Format 0_0 for the RA-Msg3 retransmission is triggered (at least the HARQ feedback of the first RA-Msg3 triggered by the RAR is Nack), the contention resolution timer is restarted.

 Upon the reception of the RA-Msg3 for an unknown UE, the U-Plane shall not schedule UL grant to resolve the contention and shall delete the temporary UE context with TC-RNTI.

 [PR879828-26R3] ; Upon the reception of the RA-Msg3 for a known RNTI, and scheduled by a DCI Format 0_0 scrambled with a C-RNTI (TC-RNTI already confirmed to a C-RNTI by a previous contention resolution (DL or UL)), the U-Plane must not update the context of the C-RNTI. For the C-RNTI context, the MAC CE is out of sequence (it is not possible to receive on a C-RNTI a MAC CE CRNTI even with the C-RNTI in it) and the MAC CEs received in the MAC-PDU for the C-RNTI should be ignored by the U-Plane. [End PR879828-26R3]

 Upon the reception of the RA-Msg3 for a known UE, the U-Plane shall delete the temporary UE context, release the TC-RNTI and schedule with UL grant(s) one uplink HARQ process to resolve the contention (including the HARQ repetitions, if needed):

 If contention resolution timer expires, the L2-PS shall not schedule UL grant for contention resolution following the RA-Msg3. If no UL DCI was sent during the contention resolution timer, at the timer expiration the Random Access procedure failure is declared for this UE.

 If contention resolution timer is running and one of the following conditions is met:

 If the HARQ process used for the contention resolution is successfully decoded (others HARQ processes can not close the contention resolution), the contention is resolved, and the RA procedure is completed.

 If only DTX was received for NRBTS/NRCELLGRP/maxUlHARQTx number of scheduling of the UL grant for contention resolution,**L2 PS shall not schedule the UL grant for contention resolution anymore.** The timer shall not be stopped.**_*_ *Rational for not stopping the timer is that PDCCH for UL contention resolution grant might have been received by UE and, thus, contention is resolved for UE.**

 If during the contention resolution, the U-Plane sends the uplink grant(s) and the HARQ process is not successfully decoded, the RA procedure is completed and only a new Random Access procedure triggered by the UE will indicate that it failed to decode the DCI used to close the contention resolution.

 For CBRA with RA-Msg3[UL-CCCH] (ex SA initial access, reestablishment...), one of the following actions is expected:

 If the contention resolution timer "conResTimer" expires which means 5G-L2-PS shall consider contention is not resolved, 5G-L2-PS shall stop the scheduling afterwards, such as RA-Msg4, DL-CCCH/DL-DCCH and also corresponding handling in 5G_UP_ALG_17940_replaced([11153286](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kslh8vvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)). ##### 3.16.5.1.0-2 CBRA contention resolution (MAC CE CRI or Uplink grant) CBRA completion procedure (MAC CE CRI) (ID: 11153010)

sendRachMsg4WithOutHIMessage = max(0, contResTimer x 2 ^ µ - margin x 2 ^ µ - addGrpPreambleMargin) 

With,

contResTimer:

µ :

Numerology accoding to the common SCS (15kHz: 0, 30kHz: 1, 120kHz: 3).

margin:

If DSS mode is inactive (NRCELL/lteNrDssMode<'DL_CRS_TDM_UL_FDM') [CB007372-AA] or (DSS is active and NRCELL.NRDSSLTE.actAsymmetricDss=true) [End CB007372-AA] or cell is not configured for SA:

 margin is equal to R&D parameter rdDssSaRachSendMsg4Margin defined in 5G_L2_3428_replaced([11187350](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_28AqUfvTEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).

addGrpPreambleMargin:

When the gNB-DU detects several preambles in a PRACH occasion, the time to trigger the delivery of the MAC control Element for the Contention Resolution Identity alone is reduced according to the number of preambles received in PRACH occasion and the preambles ordering done by lower layer as follow:

addGrpPreambleMargin= (N x (n+1)) x sfStep x 2 ^ µ

with,

### 3.16.7 Time and Frequency radio resources for the RA procedures

#### 3.16.7.0-1 Time and Frequency radio resources for the RA procedures During RA procedure, 5G-L2-PS shall not co-schedule (on the same time-frequency radio resources) any RA messages with a cell specific scrambling sequence on the same logical channel such as PDCCH, PDSCH, or PUSCH. If more than one RA message with a cell specific scrambling sequence is pending on the same logical channel, 5G-L2-PS shall time multiplex the other RA messages. This shall be independent of transmissions with a UE specific scrambling sequence on the same logical channel. Cell specific scrambling sequences based on the physCellId and UE specific ones based on the Virtual Cell ID (VCID) shall be assumed as orthogonal. (ID: 11153231)
