### 3.27.1.0-2 DL sharing pattern on FDD carrier shared with LTE DL symbols available for 5G (ID: 12774285)

**3.27.1.0-2.0-2**  (ID: `12619157`)

DL sharing pattern on FDD carrier shared with LTE On an FDD carrier shared with LTE [CB007372-A] and with NRCELL.NRDSSLTE.actAsymmetricDss=false [End CB007372-A], 5G-L2-PS shall schedule DL transmissions only on DL symbols 2, ..., 13 in DL slots available for 5G. DL symbols 0 and 1 in DL slots available for 5G must not be used because they are reserved for LTE PDCCH. 5G-L2-PS must not schedule any DL transmissions in DL slots reserved for LTE.

[CB007372-A] On an FDD carrier shared with LTE and with NRCELL.NRDSSLTE.actAsymmetricDss=true, in PRBs shared with LTE and available for 5G the 5G-L2-PS shall schedule DL transmissions only on DL symbols 2...13. Symbol#2 may be used for PDCCH or PDSCH, symbols 3..13 may be used for PDSCH. DL symbols 0 and 1 in PRBs shared with LTE, must not be used because they are reserved for LTE PDCCH. 5G-L2-PS must not schedule any DL transmissions in PRBs shared with LTE if DL slot is reserved for LTE.

In PRBs not overlapping with LTE the 5G-L2-PS may schedule in all symbols, in all slots. Symbols#0,1 may be used for PDCCH, symbol#2 may be used for PDCCH or PDSCH, symbols 3..13 may be used for PDSCH.

[End CB007372-A]

### 3.27.1.0-3 DL sharing pattern on FDD carrier shared with LTE DL sharing pattern initialisation after reception of a PsCell_CellSetupReq (ID: 11171934)

If nrLteDssMode =1, 5G-L2-PS shall set:

  * rrmFddCurrentDlShareBitmap to the 20%, 30%, 40%, 50% or 60% DL sharing pattern given in 5G_UP_ALG_2304_replaced([11171916](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kuIaFvvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) which matches the value of minMbsfnRatio.
