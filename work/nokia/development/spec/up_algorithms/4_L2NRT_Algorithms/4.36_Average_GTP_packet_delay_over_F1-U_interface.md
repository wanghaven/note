# 4.36 Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface

## 4.36.0-2 Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface Handling at gNB-CU-UP (ID: 11229645)

**4.36.0-2.0-1**  (ID: `11229651`)

Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface If NRBTS.actLatencyPmCount set to TRUE in the cloud deployment, 5G-L2-HI shall collect the following metrics for time for the computation of counter for measuring Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface.

The time of sending of DUD packet to gNB-DU at the GTP ingress termination, T1

And

The time when receiving a DDDS packet from the gNB-DU at the egress GTP termination, T4.

The Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface = ( ( T4 - T1 ) - Feedback Delay )

In the cloud deployment, the "Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface" is done for every F1-U DUD packet with poll bit sent with an interval of rdF1UDelayInterval (Note 1).

5G-L2-HI stores the timestamp and the NR-U SN of the DUD with poll bit sent for the purpose of the computation of the F1-U packet delay and if there is no feedback delay received in the next two DDDS for that PDU, the time stamp will be cleaned up and the delay computation is not done for the DUD packet.

The DUD polling for the bearers for the purpose of F1-U delay measurement should follow the polling period, when DDDS reduction is enabled.

Note:

  1. The Feedback Delay is received in the corresponding DDDS message for which the DUD with poll bit is sent. In the cloud deployment, polling is done as per the DDDSperiod configuration where as the measurement computation is executed every rdF1UDelayInterval period.



The measurement is performed per PLMN ID, per QoS level and per S-NSSAI.

Refer 5G_UP_ALG_26093_replaced([11229670](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kyptsfvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)) for more details.

rdF1UDelayInterval is a R&D parameter defined in 5G_UP_ALG_26098_replaced([11229693](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kypttPvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ)).

## 4.36.0-3 Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface Handling at gNB-DU (ID: 11229658)

**4.36.0-3.0-1**  (ID: `11229664`)

Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface If NRBTS.actLatencyPmCount set to TRUE, 5G-L2-HI shall collect the following metrics for the computation of Feedback Delay Time for the computation of counter for measuring Average GTP packet delay over F1-U interface.

The time of reception of DUD packet with poll bit, T2

And

The time of creation of response DDDS after processing the DUD with poll bit, T3

The Feedback Delay = T3 - T2

The computed Feedback Delay and the NR-U SN of the F1-U DUD are populated in the corresponding F1-U DDDS response message as Feedback Delay Result and NR-U Sequence Number of Polling Frame.

This measurement is obtained as:

  * the time when receiving a GTP packet from the gNB-DU at the egress GTP termination (feedback receiving point at the PDCP - DDDS), minus
  * time when sending the same packet to gNB-DU at the GTP ingress termination (Sending the PDCP packet from PDCP - DUD), minus
  * feedback delay time in gNB-DU.



The measurement is performed per PLMN ID and per QoS level (mapped 5QI or QCI in NR option 3) and per S-NSSAI.

NOTE : The NR RAN container (DL USER DATA/ DL DATA DELIVERY STATUS) carried in the GTP-U packet over the F1-U interface is used for the measurement.

At PDCP: DUD sent at T1 and DDDS received at T4

At RLC: DUD received at T2, process the DUD with poll bit and respond with a DDDS at T3 filling the IE: Feedback Delay Result
