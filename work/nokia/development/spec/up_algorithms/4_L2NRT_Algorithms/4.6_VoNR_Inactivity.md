# 4.6 VoNR Inactivity

## 4.6.0-2 VoNR Inactivity Inactivity Period for VoNR (ID: 11223896)

**4.6.0-2.0-2**  (ID: `11223908`)

VoNR Inactivity DL inactivity period for VoNR

The time period of DL inactivity is the time difference between "the time instant when downlink [Before CNI-116870] PDCP SDU buffer transition from empty to non-empty" and "time instant of F1-U DDDS reception with Highest transmitted PDCP SN = TX_NEXT -1"

TX_NEXT is PDCP state variable as defined in 5G_UP_IF_3768_replaced([11533502](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_tW3ckfv1Ee-AqvopbP1qhQ))

[CNI-116870] PDCP PDU containing a VONR RTP packet is sent to the DU till the next PDCP PDU containing VONR RTP packet is sent to the DU till bearer is deleted. [End CNI-116870]

UL inactivity period for VoNR

The time period of UL inactivity is the time difference between reception of consecutive uplink PDCP PDU from gNB-DU.

[CNI-116870] The UL inactivity calculation starts upon sending first RTP packet in the UL to core. This UL VoNR inactive duration counter is the "time difference between consecutive uplink RTP media Packetstransmitted to core”. Packets other than RTP packets shall be ignored from the calculation. [End CNI-116870]
