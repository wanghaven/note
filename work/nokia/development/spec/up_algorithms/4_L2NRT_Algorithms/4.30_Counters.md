**4.30.1.0-1.0-1.0-1.0-3**  (ID: `12727022`)

UE throughput counters
Upon reception of the activation flag ( NRBTS.actIpThpt ) for enabling the throughput counting for the classical BTS and the cloud BTS, gNB-CU-UP shall provide counters at given aggregation levels to count the volume and time using data bursts for the computation of DL throughput.

[Before CNI-134962-C]

The start of a burst is the point in time the first portion of the data is sent over air to UE, i.e. the burst time shall exclude the initial scheduling / retention delay.

The end of burst is identified when last portion of the data sent over air to UE which empties the buffer for the given DRB.

During bearer setup and bearer modification 5G-L2-HI shall receive the "Cell ID" for the bearer in the message which shall be used for cell level aggregation of volume and time counters.

**Volume Counter**

This counter counts the PDCP SDU level volume **(refer *NOTE 2) \*\*of every data burst transmitted to gNB-DU from all bearers that belongs to one QoS group and to a cell.***\*\*

**Volume Counter per PMQAP Profile**

For the classical BTS, this counter counts the PDCP PDU level volume of every data burst transmitted from all bearers that belong to one PMQAP profile for 5G Leg and LTE Leg separately.

**Time Counter**

This counter counts the time to transmit a PDCP SDU level data burst  (refer *NOTE 2) transmitted to gNB-DU from all bearers that belongs to one QoS group and to a cell.  The estimation of the time for the transmission of a burst is based on 3GPP based input information of DDDS message (last recevied), such as DDR.  In case the Desired Data Rate is not received in the DDDS message, 5G-L2- HI can use the instantaneous estimated throughput based on the data outflow and the duration between DDDS messages, as DDR.*

Time needed to transmit (ms) = Total size of PDCP PDUs of the data burst (Kbits) / Desired Data Rate (Kbits / ms)

For the classical BTS, this counter counts the time to transmit a PDCP PDU level data burst transmitted from all bearers that belong to one PMQAP profile for 5G Leg and LTE Leg separately.

**Max DL Throughput Counter**

This counter provides the maximum burst throughput of DL PDCP bursts transmitted during the measurement period per cell.

**Exclusion of Small Volume and Short Session Bursts:**

Small volume and short session bursts (as configured by parameters given below received from C Plane) are excluded from the counting.  After end of each burst if the Volume or Time count value is less than the corresponding thresholds given below then the volume and time count value is not added to increment the corresponding counter.

*volThresDiscard is data burst volume threshold to discard the counting results.*

*durationThresDiscard is data burst duration threshold to discard the counting results.*

For the PMQAP profile volume and time counters, the threshold values are provided separately for 5G Leg and LTE Leg as given below:

*NRBTS.NRDRB.volThresDiscardDlPdcpPdu5GLeg*

*NRBTS.NRDRB.durationThresDiscardDlPdcpPdu5GLeg*

*NRBTS.NRDRB.volThresDiscardDlPdcpPduLteLeg*

*NRBTS.NRDRB.durationThresDiscardDlPdcpPduLteLeg*

*NOTE:*

1) Separate counters are provided for 5G Leg (applicable for SA scenario also), LTE Leg and Combined 5G + LTE.

2) **Only new PDCP SDUs / PDUs shall be considered (i.e.** PDCP retransmitted PDUs/ PDCP PDUs marked for retransmission are excluded)**.**\*\*\*\*

[CNI-134962-C]

Cell level counters:

Cell ID is used for aggregation of counters across all bearers belonging to the cell. Throughput is computed for each DRB and aggregated to the cell using the volume and time counters. During bearer setup and bearer modification gNB-CU-UP shall receive the "Cell ID" for the bearer in the message which shall be used for cell level aggregation of volume and time counters.

Burst handling for each DRB shall be handled as described in [12726202: Burst handling](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_WXPUYXD_EfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)  
Volume counter for each DRB shall be counted for each DRB as described in [12726199: Volume counter](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_Pk_kgXD_EfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)  and cumulated for each cell.  
Time counter for each DRB shall be counted for each DRB as described in [12726201: Time counter](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_TIrGonD_EfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9) and cumulated for each cell.

**Max DL Throughput Counter**

This counter provides the maximum burst throughput of DL PDCP valid bursts transmitted (validity of the burst determined as per section [12726202: Burst handling](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_WXPUYXD_EfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)) per cell level during the measurement period.

QoS level counters:

5QI/QCI information is used for aggregation of counters across all bearers belonging to the QoS group. Throughput is computed for each DRB and aggregated to the QoS group using the volume and time counters. During bearer setup and bearer modification gNB-CU-UP shall receive the 5QI/QCI information for the bearer in the message which shall be used for QoS level aggregation of volume and time counters.

gNB-CU-UP shall cumulate throughput counters using the [12726188: DRB level counters](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_uvI-QHD9EfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9) section for each DRB under specific QoS group.  
Burst handling for each DRB shall be handled as described in [12726202: Burst handling](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_WXPUYXD_EfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)  
Volume counter for each DRB shall be counted as described in [12726199: Volume counter](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_Pk_kgXD_EfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9) and cumulated for each QoS group.  
Time counter for each DRB shall be counted as described in [12726201: Time counter](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_TIrGonD_EfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9) and cumulated for each QoS group.

PMQAP level counters:

"PMQAP Profile ID" is used for aggregation of counters across all bearers belonging to the PMQAP profile. Throughput is computed for each DRB and aggregated to the configured PMQAP profile using the volume and time counters. During bearer setup and bearer modification gNB-CU-UP shall receive the "PMQAP Profile ID" for the bearer in the message which shall be used for PMQAP level aggregation of volume and time counters.

gNB-CU-UP shall be cumulate volume and time for each DRB using [12728583: DRB level counters](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_arUkaHHeEfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9) section for the configured PMQAP profile.  
Burst handling for each configured PMQAP profile shall be handled as described in [12726531: Burst handling](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_A6UTQnEUEfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)  
Volume counter for each configured PMQAP profile shall be counted as described in [12726523: Volume counter](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_tw1g8XETEfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)  
Time counter for each configured PMQAP profile shall be counted as described in [12728580: Time Counter](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_6cvkEnHdEfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)

**4.30.1.0-2.0-2.0-1.0-2**  (ID: `12730643`)

UE throughput counters
Upon reception of the activation flag for enabling the throughput counting for DL RLC PDU Throughput (*NRBTS.actMacUserThpt, received from C Plane), gNB-DU shall provide volume and time counters for the combined bursts at RLC PDU level computed from the volume and time data of bursts reported by all 5G-L2-LO instances.*

This volume and time counters are provided for the computation of combined DL RLC PDU throughput of all 5G-L2-LO instances and is reported separately per cell level as well as per PMQAP level.

For the computation of the combined DL RLC PDU throughput, the retransmitted RLC PDUs are treated same as new RLC PDUs.

**Computation of the combined bursts at RLC PDU level**

The algorithm details for combining the bursts and the computation of ThpVolDl and ThpTimeDl using the volume data TotVol and LastVol and the time data t0, t1, t2, t3 and t4 provided by each 5G-L2-LO instance with an example is given in 5G\_UP\_ALG\_19549\_replaced([11228573: Burst Combining for DL RLC PDU Throughput Counter](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_kyjACvvSEe-AqvopbP1qhQ?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)).

[Before CNI-134962-C]

**The Volume Counter**

This counter counts the RLC PDU level burst volume of a combined data burst, ThpVolDl,

- excluding the volume transmitted in the last slot

- per PMQAP Profile and per cell

- after combining all the bursts reported for each bearer by all 5G-L2-LO instances.

5G-L2-HI receives following volume data for each DRB from each 5G-L2-LO instance for a burst transmitted from the 5G-L2-LO instance to the UE.

TotVol = Total volume of all DL RLC PDU's of the burst that is transmitted over the air to the UE by the 5G-L2-LO instance.

LastVol = Volume of the last part DL RLC PDU of the burst transmitted over the air to the UE by the 5G-L2-LO instance.

**The Time Counter**

This counter counts the time to transmit a DL RLC PDU level data burst, ThpTimeDl,

- excluding the time to transmit the last slot

- per PMQAP Profile and per cell

- after combining all the bursts reported for each bearer by all 5G-L2-LO instances.

5G-L2-HI receives following time data for each DRB from each 5G-L2-LO instance for a burst transmitted from the 5G-L2-LO instance to the UE.

t0 = point in time when the 1st DL PDU is received at 5G-L2-LO from 5G-L2-HI DU.

t1 = Point in time when first TB of the block transmission started over air to UE

t2 = Point in time when second last TB of the block transmitted over air to UE.

t3 = Point in time when Payload built completed at 5G-L2-LO for last TB of block

t4 = Point in time when last TB of the block transmitted over air to UE.

**Exclusion of Small Volume and Short Duration Bursts:**

Small volume and short duration bursts (as configured by parameters given below received from C Plane) are excluded from the counting.  After end of computation of the combined burst information, if the Volume or Time count value is less than the corresponding thresholds given below then the volume and time count value is not added to increment the corresponding counters.

*NRBTS.NRDRB.volThresDiscardDlMacSdu is data burst volume threshold to discard the counting results.*

*NRBTS.NRDRB.durationThresDiscardDlMacSdu is data burst duration threshold to discard the counting results.*

[CNI-134962-C]

Burst handling for each DRB shall be handled as described in [12727923: Burst handling](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_hc3TAHHFEfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9)

Volume counter for each DRB shall be counted as described in [12727918: Volume counter](https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/rm/resources/BI_baikMXHFEfCRE7eh4Ndkrw?oslc_config.context=https://dn-prod.ext.net.nokia.com/gc/configuration/9) for all bearers in a cell and for configured PMQAP profile.

The start of a burst is the point in time the first portion of the data is sent over air to UE, i.e. the burst time shall exclude the initial scheduling / retention delay.

The end of burst is identified when last portion of the data sent over air to UE which empties the buffer for the given DRB.

NRBTS.NRDRB.volThresDiscardDlMacSdu is data burst volume threshold to discard the counting results.

NRBTS.NRDRB.durationThresDiscardDlMacSdu is data burst duration threshold to discard the counting results.

Small volume and short duration bursts (as configured by parameters received from C Plane) are excluded from the counting.  After end of computation of the combined burst information, if the volume or time count value is higher than the corresponding thresholds then the volume and time count value is added to the PM counter measurement, otherwise, it is discarded.

NR Carrier Aggregation Counters
Upon reception of the activation flag for enabling the per CC UE throughput counting in the downlink (NRBTS.actPerCCDLUserThpt, received from C Plane), 5G-L2-HI DU shall provide volume and time counters for each CC combination at DL RLC PDU level, based on the data of the combined burst.

The combined bursts at RLC PDU level are computed from the volume and duration of local bursts reported by all 5G-L2-LO instances as described in 5G\_UP\_ALG\_19450.

5G-L2-HI DU does the following computations to enable additional per CC UE throughput counters in addition to the procedures as described in 5G\_UP\_ALG\_19450.

Whenever there is a change in the CC combination (e.g. change in the number of secondary 5G-L2-LO paths used for transmitting the data), 5G-L2-HI DU shall calculate the time duration of each CC combination based on overlapping of the local bursts as reported by 5G-L2-LO instances as described in 5G\_UP\_ALG\_19450.  At the end of the combined burst, 5G-L2-HI DU computes the volume of each CC combination employed in the burst by multiplying the time duration of the CC combination and the throughput and they are added to get the per CC combination volume and time duration.

The algorithm details for the computation of volume and time data for each CC combination is described with an example in 5G\_UP\_ALG\_25544.

Data sent through Primary and Secondary 5G-L2-LO paths are pegged.

Only non-GBR bearers considered in this counters, GBR bearers are excluded.

Small volume and short session bursts (as configured in NRBTS.NRDRB.volThresDiscardDlMacSdu & NRBTS.NRDRB.durationThresDiscardDlMacSdu are excluded from the counting as described in 5G\_UP\_ALG\_19450.

Last slot exclusion and discard parameter-based filtering are applied on merged burst. They are not applied to each CC combination burst.  When a merged burst is discarded due to small volume or short duration, per CC data counters for that burst also are discarded.

Upon reception of the activation flag for enabling the per CC UE throughput counting in the downlink (NRBTS.actPerCCDLUserThpt, received from C Plane), 5G-L2-HI DU shall provide the UE Throughput per Mhz BW.

The combined bursts at RLC PDU level are computed from the volume and time data of bursts reported by all 5G-L2-LO instances as described in 5G\_UP\_ALG\_19450.

At the end of each P(S)Cell merged burst, DurationBW is calculated by multiplying duration of each local burst with bandwidth of component carrier cell which carried that local burst.

The bandwidth used here is of dedicated BWP which is used by UE in that component carrier cell, as given by the CPlane.

UE Throughput at P(S)Cell per Mhz = (Total volume of the merged burst) / DurationBW

When a merged burst is discarded for small volume or small duration as given in 5G\_UP\_ALG\_19450,  DurationBW  is not pegged for that burst.
